# FUTURE_DS_03

Bank Marketing Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview:

This project presents an interactive Bank Marketing Analysis Dashboard built in Power BI to evaluate the effectiveness of bank marketing campaigns and identify customer segments with the highest conversion rates. The dashboard enables stakeholders to analyze customer behavior, campaign performance, and demographic trends to improve marketing strategies and increase customer conversions.

Objectives:
Analyze customer conversion performance.
Identify customer segments most likely to subscribe to bank products.
Evaluate campaign effectiveness across different customer demographics.
Support data-driven marketing decisions through interactive visualizations.

Key Metrics:
Total Customers: 41.19K
Customers Converted: 5K
Conversion Rate: 11.27%
Average Campaign Contacts: 2.57

Dashboard Insights:
Customer Conversion by Job
Customers working in Administrative, Technician, and Blue-Collar professions contribute the highest number of conversions.
These job segments represent key target audiences for future campaigns.

Customer Conversion by Education:
Customers with a University Degree show the highest conversion rates.
High School and Professional Course customers also contribute significantly to conversions.
Educational background plays an important role in campaign response behavior.

Customer Conversion by Previous Campaign Outcome:
Customers with a successful previous interaction are more likely to convert again.
Previous campaign outcomes can be used to improve customer targeting and retention strategies.

Customer Conversion by Age Category:
The 26–50 age group represents the largest share of converted customers.
Customers aged 51–75 also demonstrate strong conversion performance.
Younger and older age groups contribute comparatively fewer conversions.

Loan-Based Analysis:
The dashboard enables comparison between customers with and without existing loans.
This helps evaluate how financial obligations influence marketing campaign responses.

Time-Based Analysis:
Interactive filters allow users to analyze campaign performance by:
Day of the week
Month
Loan status
These filters help identify the most effective periods for customer outreach.

Tools & Technologies:
Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Data Modeling
Data Visualization

Business Recommendations:
Focus marketing efforts on customers aged 26–50.
Prioritize highly responsive job categories such as administrative and technical professionals.
Leverage previous campaign success data for targeted remarketing.
Design personalized campaigns for customers based on educational background.
Optimize campaign schedules using day and month performance analysis.

Outcome:
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of customer conversion patterns and campaign performance, enabling banks to improve targeting strategies, increase conversion rates, and maximize marketing ROI.

Skills Demonstrated: Power BI, Data Cleaning, Data Modeling, DAX Measures, KPI Development, Dashboard Design, Marketing Analytics, Customer Segmentation, Business Intelligence, Data Visualization.
